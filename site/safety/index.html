Token safety check

Paste a Polygon token address. Triviu runs a real on-chain buy → sell simulation and tells you whether you could actually get your money back out — the single most common trap is a token you can buy but cannot sell. It runs in your browser and sends no transaction.

Try: WETH (safe) · paste any Polygon token traded against WMATIC on QuickSwap.
This is a heuristic on-chain screen, not a guarantee. It simulates a buy and a sell against QuickSwap right now — it catches honeypots and transfer taxes, but it cannot know if the owner will change the rules later, nor does it audit the contract. A pass here is not "safe forever." Don't trust this — verify it yourself.
