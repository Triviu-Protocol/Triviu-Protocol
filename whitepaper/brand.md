# Brand

The Triviu brand exists for one thing: **to make the verifiable visible.** Everything —
from the logotype to the color of a button — derives from the whitepaper, the principles,
and the blockchain trilemma as a structure of honesty. Brand assets are **CC BY 4.0**.

## The mark — "The Cycle"

Three equal nodes on a rotated, open cycle — **no base, no apex, no hierarchy.** It is
the triangular route (A→B→C→A) and the trilemma at once: three forces held in tension,
none dominant. The rule is deliberate — **never draw the perfect closed triangle.** The
perfect triangle is the perfect lie; the open cycle admits the cost of every choice.

The three nodes carry the brand triad — ultramar, lacre, açafrão — the same three that
mark the three axes of the trilemma throughout the docs.

Dark mode mirrors these tokens. The rule from the trilemma holds in color too: never
plot 1.0 on all three axes — the palette states costs, it doesn't hide them.

## Typography

- **Libre Caslon Text** — display / headings. A serif with authority and calm; it reads
  as a founding document, not a pitch.
- **Public Sans** — body. Neutral, legible, public-domain-spirited.
- **IBM Plex Mono** — numbers, labels, code, the `AI-GENERATED` badge. Where precision
  must look like precision.

## Voice & writing

Precise, honest, measured. Education before execution. **Zero hype, zero promise.** The
brand's north star is the protocol's: *don't trust, verify.*

- **Say:** open, non-custodial, verifiable, atomic, the fee wall, possibility is not
  probability, education not income, failures included.
- **Never say:** guaranteed, returns, passive income, risk-free, revolutionary,
  game-changer, "to the moon", or any word that turns a tool into a promise.
- Every number carries a source. Every claim is checkable. No self-praise without a link.
- English is canonical; Spanish is the only toggle. No other language, no regional framing.

## Governance
The brand changes only by pull request, like the protocol. A visual or copy decision
that can't be traced to the whitepaper or the principles doesn't ship.
