# 10. Education and the AI persona

All of Triviu's educational content is presented by a synthetic persona,
identified as AI in every channel biography and in every video or post. The
"AI-GENERATED" label is a mandatory component, never removed or hidden — both a
principle and, where applicable, a legal-transparency requirement.

## The curriculum — four pillars

1. **AMMs and pools from zero.** How pools set prices (the constant-product
   formula), why discrepancies exist, and what happens to price when you trade.
2. **Anatomy of a real cycle.** One real triangular arbitrage, block by block,
   with actual numbers — gas, slippage, competition — and what a professional
   searcher sees versus what an individual sees.
3. **Run it yourself.** Install Foundry, spin up a fork, load the parameters, run
   the engine in dry-run, and read every line of what the simulator outputs.
4. **Wallet and MEV literacy.** Key safety, approval hygiene, and how MEV bots
   actually win — and why an individual often does not.

## Non-negotiable editorial rule

Show technology, never income. No Triviu material displays profit projections,
earnings screenshots or get-rich language. If a piece cannot survive the question
*can the viewer verify this themselves?*, it does not ship.
