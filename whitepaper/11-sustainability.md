# 11. Sustainability

The protocol funds itself primarily through the on-chain
[success fee](05-success-fee.md) — a share of the profit on cycles that actually
profit, taken atomically, capped in bytecode, and emitted for anyone to audit.
This is complemented by:

- **Ecosystem grants** (Polygon and similar).
- **On-chain donations** to a public address, with a public balance.
- **B2B technical services** — integration and consulting.

## What Triviu will never do for money

There are no paid signals, no premium groups, no third-party capital management,
and no product that depends on user deposits. The success fee is protocol revenue
on a **settled result** — not a fee on custody, not a fee on a promise, not a fee
on entry. If a revenue idea would require holding a user's funds or promising a
return, it is out of bounds by construction.
