§ 01

Foundations

The Triviu brand exists for one thing: to make the verifiable visible. Everything it communicates — from the logotype to the color of a button — derives from the litepaper, the seven principles and, as of this version, the blockchain trilemma as a structure of honesty.

1.1Essence

"The ability to verify."

We do not sell an outcome; we deliver the means to check it. Every piece must survive this question: can the reader verify this on their own? If not, the piece does not ship.

1.2The name

Triviu derives from the Latin trivium — the meeting of three roads, and also the classical curriculum that founded all education. Spelling: always "Triviu", capital T. Suggested pronunciation: /ˈtrɪviu/.

§ 02

The trilemma as structure

The blockchain trilemma says no network maximizes decentralization, security and scalability at once: improving two axes charges a price on the third. The industry usually treats it as a marketing problem — "we solved the trilemma". Triviu does the opposite: it adopts the trilemma as an instrument of honesty and builds it into the brand itself.

2.1Triviu's position

"Triviu doesn't solve the trilemma. It travels it — and documents the price of every lap."

The classic trilemma is drawn as a static triangle: pick two vertices and accept losing the third. Our mark draws it as a cycle: with each release, the protocol moves along the three axes, declares which axis it prioritized and which one paid — in the Tradeoff Record (§ 07.7). Choosing is inevitable; hiding the choice is optional. We don't hide it.

2.2The mark, read through the trilemma

Plate 1 — The three readings overlaid: the nodes are the pools (A·B·C) and the trilemma vertices. The flow does not elect one vertex: it circulates through all three. Traveled, not chosen.

2.3Our founding record — Polygon PoS

TRADEOFF RECORD No. 0001 · FOUNDING DECISION

This record opens the series. Every published architecture decision gets its own — rule in § 07.7.

§ 03

Mark & logotype — "The Cycle"

Three equal nodes joined by directional arcs, with no side resting on the ground. A pyramid is static hierarchy; this is flow that ends where it began — atomicity, the traveled trilemma and the trivium, in a single form.

Plate 2 — Inscribed equilateral triangle, rotated 90°: no side rests. Arcs with sagitta f = R/7 in the A→B→C→A direction; arrows 120° apart; nodes of radius x = R/5. The cycle closes where it began. Verifiable, therefore proven.

§ 04

Color

The palette is a proof-plate triad — blue, red and yellow over paper and ink, in the tradition of the proof books (Byrne, Elements of Euclid, 1847). In v2.0 it gains a function: each color is a trilemma axis. None of them is "profit green"; that is deliberate.

Plate 3 — The palette is the cycle and the trilemma: each axis, one color; each color, one journey. Ultramar → Lacre → Açafrão → Ultramar.

§ 05

Typography

Three voices, three origins — all open source, as principle 2 demands. The rule of three runs through the system: three colors, three families, three readings, three axes.

Display — Libre Caslon Text
To decentralize, to secure, to scale — and to document the price of each verb.

Caslon is the letterform of the 19th-century proof books — the same tradition as the plates. Use: headlines, cover, quotations. Weights 400 and 700; italic in quotations and the essence.

Text — Public Sans
Created as the U.S. government's public typeface and maintained in an open repository, Public Sans carries in its name what Triviu stands for: what is public can be verified. Use: body, interface, buttons. Weights 400–700.
Data — IBM Plex Mono
block 58 214 002 · route A→B→C→A · 0x7a3f…c2d9 · minProfit = 0.0031

Everything on-chain is set in mono: addresses, hashes, blocks, parameters, table figures. Addresses truncated in the 0x1234…abcd pattern (4+4), with a typographic ellipsis.

§ 06

Design tokens & interface principles

This document consumes the very tokens it specifies — inspect the CSS and check. What follows applies to the site, docs, dashboard and any protocol frontend.

6.1Space, radius and hairline

GroupScaleRule

Radius4 · 6 · 10 · pillSubtle. Sharp corners only in geometric plates.

ElevationnoneTransparency casts no shadow. Depth is made with hairlines and background color.

6.2Motion — the 120° rule

As sober as everything else: 150–200ms, ease-out, only on hover, focus and reveal. Mark rotations happen only in multiples of 120° — one node of the cycle at a time, always in the A→B→C→A direction (hover the cover mark). Nothing bounces, nothing pulses: hype in motion is still hype. Always respect prefers-reduced-motion.

6.3Iconography

1.75px stroke, pure geometry, flat terminals, 24px grid, one color per icon. Forbidden: coin, vault, rocket and bull/bear icons.

§ 07

Components

Rendered live with this document's tokens. Three are mandatory by principle: the AI badge, the risk notice and — new in v2.0 — the Tradeoff Record.

7.1Buttons

The label says exactly what happens ("Simulate on a fork", never "Start earning"). Primary action in Ultramar; one per screen.

7.2Badges

AI-GENERATED ✓ VERIFIED ON-CHAIN TESTNET

7.3AI badge — mandatory

All AI-generated content carries the "AI-GENERATED" badge: Tinta background, Papel text in Plex Mono, Violeta-Polygon dot. Placement: top-right corner in video and imagery, and in the piece's footer. Never removed, never scaled below 24px in height, never hidden in a description.

7.4Risk notice — mandatory

RISK NOTICE — REQUIRED READING

Most atomic-arbitrage opportunities on Polygon are captured by professional operators. For most individual users, the expected result after gas costs tends toward zero or negative. Triviu is educational infrastructure — it is not a source of income. Reverted transactions still pay gas.

Fixed copy, one official translation per language, no paraphrasing. Present on: home page, execution docs, README, every persona video description.

A metric without its failure context is marketing; a metric with failures is evidence. We only publish the latter.

7.7Tradeoff Record — mandatory

Every published architecture decision ships with its record: three lines, one per trilemma axis, each with a verdict — GAINS HOLDS or COSTS. A record without a cost line is invalid — if nothing was paid, nothing was decided. The canonical example is Record No. 0001 (§ 2.3); the series continues, numbered, in the repository.

7.8Trilemma diagram

Reading of Record No. 0001 — Polygon PoS: Sc 0.9 · S 0.8 · D 0.55. Component rule: plotting 1.0 on all three axes is forbidden — the perfect triangle is the perfect lie.
§ 08

Voice & writing

Triviu's voice is that of someone who demonstrates, not someone who persuades: clear, technical, calm and promise-free. Non-negotiable editorial rule: show technology, never income. And, in v2.0: name the tradeoff, never deny it.

8.1Vocabulary

Forbidden — in any pieceUse instead
"passive income", "guaranteed profit", "earn $X a day""simulate", "verify", "read the code"
"we solved the trilemma", "the best of all three worlds", "no tradeoffs""our position on the trilemma" + Tradeoff Record link
Dollar signs, money emoji, earnings screenshots, profit countersBlock numbers, hashes, revert rate, gas
"once-in-a-lifetime opportunity", "limited spots", artificial urgencyRelease dates, changelog, roadmap without promised deadlines
"trust us", "safe and guaranteed""don't trust: verify", link to the verified contract
Return projections, backtests as promisesBacktests as method, with code and data to reproduce

8.2Right and wrong

WRONG"Meet the bot that earns on the blockchain while you sleep! 🚀💰"
RIGHT"What happens inside an arbitrage transaction — block by block, with the contract open on screen."
WRONG"Polygon solved the trilemma: fast, secure and decentralized."
RIGHT"We chose Polygon for scalability — and Record No. 0001 documents what that costs in decentralization."
WRONG"Proven results: +3% a day on our protocol."
RIGHT"Revert rate over the last 30 days: 37.2%. Public dashboard, failures included."

8.3Tone by channel

Docs and litepaper: formal-technical, declarative sentences. Persona videos: professorial and direct, always with the AI badge in frame. Social: concise, one verifiable fact per post, link to the evidence. Everywhere: plain language, jargon only when defined first — "trilemma" included.

9.4Publication checklist

No piece ships without passing every item:

  • Zero income promises, projections or profit screenshots — including implicit ones.
  • No "trilemma solved" claims; if the piece cites an architecture decision, the Tradeoff Record is linked — cost line filled in.
  • "AI-GENERATED" badge present and legible whenever AI-generated content appears.
  • Risk notice (§ 7.4) present whenever the piece touches execution.
  • Every technical claim has a verifiable link (contract, block, PR or public data).
  • Palette, typography, rotation and clear space per §§ 3–5 — the mark never rests on a base.
  • No dollar sign, coin, rocket, bull or bear in the iconography.
§ 10

Brand governance

The brand follows the same flow as the protocol: public, versioned and changeable by merit.

The assets (SVGs, tokens, this manual) live in the repository's /brand directory, under CC BY 4.0. Changes come in via pull request with rationale; Git history is the official record. Forks and integrations that remove the risk notice, the AI badge or the tradeoff records violate the spirit of the project and may not use the Triviu name.
