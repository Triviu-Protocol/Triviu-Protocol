## Files

- `simbolo.svg` — full mark, with arrows (use at 40px and above)
- `simbolo-min.svg` — no arrows (24–40px, favicon, badges)
- `simbolo-mono.svg` — monochrome Ink
- `simbolo-negativo.svg` — for dark backgrounds (Ink #16181D)

## Quick rules (the manual has the details)

- Clear space: 2x (one node diameter) on all sides.
- Never rest the mark on a horizontal base; never mirror it (reverses A->B->C->A).
- Never recolor it "profit green", add shadows, or pair it with dollar signs.
- The "AI-GENERATED" badge and the Risk Notice are mandatory components.

Forks that remove the risk notice, the AI label or the tradeoff records may
not use the Triviu name.
