# Tradeoff Record No. 0005 — Expansion network: BNB Smart Chain (BSC)

- **Date:** July 2026
- **Status:** accepted (expansion candidate — pre-sanctioned by Record 0001)
- **Originating PR:** multi-chain propagation onda

## Decision

Triviu extends to BNB Smart Chain as an EVM execution target. The Solidity
contracts are the **same** (BSC is EVM-compatible); only a chain configuration is
added (`engine/config/params.bsc.example.toml`). Polygon PoS (Record 0001) remains
the default and reference chain.

Deployment remains gated by the mainnet audit process. Nothing is deployed on any
chain at v0. The simulator models BSC today with real, on-chain-verified pools.

We record BSC's cost honestly: it buys reach and deep stablecoin liquidity at the
price of **more decentralization than Polygon**, not less. A user choosing BSC
should know that.

## Trilemma reading

| Axis | Verdict | Rationale and mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Scalability | **GAINS** | Very low gas and high throughput — cycles and mistakes are cheap, good for learning. |
| Security | **HOLDS** | Atomicity removes leg exposure — the cycle closes or reverts. Contract risk unchanged (same audited code). Chain-level security is weaker than an Ethereum-settled chain. |
| Decentralization | **COSTS MORE (than Polygon)** | A small active validator set closely associated with a single company. This is the most centralized of the three EVM targets — stated plainly, not hidden. Mitigation: verified contracts, local-fork simulation, self-hosted RPC; the honest mitigation is disclosure. |

## Alternatives considered

Not adding BSC (rejected — it holds the deepest retail stablecoin liquidity, which
matters for education reach); other high-throughput chains (future records if
adopted).

## Consequences

The engine reads BSC via config; the DEX venue is PancakeSwap V2, whose pool fee is
**0.25% (`fee_bps = 25`), not 0.30%** — the engine reads `fee_bps` per pool, so the
`0.997` shortcut does not apply on BSC. BSC's USDT and USDC use **18 decimals**
(not 6). Addresses verified on-chain 2026-07-19 (router → factory → pool
consistent); re-verify on bscscan.com. Trilemma-diagram reading: S 0.90 · Sec 0.75 · D 0.35.
